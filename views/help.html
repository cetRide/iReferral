Patient account

To create an account as a patient, fill in the form provided when you click on a button "Patient portal" and create account link on the dropdown provided.The password sould be eight or more characters with a combination of both upper and lower case plus digits

To log in to the system click on the sign in link and provide the huduma number in its respective field and the password you created during the sign up.

Doctor's account

To create an account as a patient, fill in the form provided when you click on a button "Doctor portal" and create profile link on the dropdown provided.The password sould be eight or more characters with a combination of both upper and lower case plus digits

To log in to the system click on the sign in link and provide the employee id provided by the hospital management in its respective field and the password you created during the sign up.

Hospital management

On the landing page("home page") on the left side of the navigation bar, click on the "hospital management" button to get authenticated

To create an account click on register and provide the fields to the form displayed.

To log in fill in the two fields of the form with national id and password you created during registration

Having further problems, write to us.

Referrals can only be viewed by the patient and hospital management.

Log in to your account as a patient or the hospital management.

Patient referrals

As a patient, input in the forms provided with the start date and end dates (this are the dates you wish to view your referrals within those days and click search.

Hospital referrals

As a hospital administrator, input in the forms provided with the start date and end dates (this are the dates you wish to view the referrals conducted to your facility within those days and click search.

Find patient is always done by the doctor when the patient needs to be diagnosed.

You are required first to log in to the system as a doctor.

On the doctor's dashboard, click on the "Find patient" button and searcch the patient by the huduma number.

Referrals are done by the doctor.

To refer a patient, click on the refer patient link on the doctor's dashboard, fill the form that pops up with the patient huduma number and the service name the patient requires to undergo.

Upon submission of the valid data, another page appears, where you're required to choose the desired hospital.Select date and time the patient is required to be referred.Then submit the details of the form.
